Welcome to Thotilla!

“Thotilla” is a baby cradle which take care of baby’s action and notify parents even parents are not near the baby. Mainly this cradle will give parents time to do their work without worrying about baby. “Thotilla” will notify parents when baby is awake, when baby is crying, when baby wet their nappies and when baby going to fall as well. Also “Thotilla” is able to swing the cradle, play music until baby fall sleep and also it is capable of keep baby without crying until parents come near to baby by playing a toy automatically. Also Thotilla is capable of connect with parents mobile in order to keep baby and parents interact by allowing parent to see and hear baby through phone and also to baby to see and hear parents as well.
